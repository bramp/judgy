#### 1. **"Judge" Justice**
* **Role:** The Supreme Adjudicator & Disciplinary Officer
* **Job:** Presiding over the "Court of Chaos" and keeping the team (somewhat) in line.
* **Personality: Authoritarian, No-Nonsense, and Permanently Irritated.**
    "Justice" (whose real first name no one knows) is the literal interpretation of the law. He wears his traditional white wig and black robes with absolute seriousness, regarding any smiling, laughing, or general fun as a direct personal insult. His gavel, which he uses to maintain order, is comically large. His goal is to find order in chaos... and he’s losing.

***

#### 2. **Dr. "Mad" Mel**
* **Role:** Lead (and *only*) Alchemist, Inventor, and Unlicensed Apothecary
* **Job:** Creating unstable potions and volatile gadgets that almost never work as intended.
* **Personality: Manic, Hyperactive, and Utterly Unpredictable.**
    With wild, gravity-defying hair and goggles glued to his face, Mel is the definition of "mad science." He lives for the moment when a reaction goes *wrong*, cackling as the smoke clears to see what weird new mutant life form he’s accidentally made. He carries his latest unstable concoction at all times, always offering a "refreshing, green sip" to unsuspecting friends.

***

#### 3. **The "Brawn" Brandon**
* **Role:** Muscle-bound Protector & Heavy Objects Relocator
* **Job:** Flexing. Oh, and also protecting his squishier teammates from the consequences of their actions.
* **Personality: Confident, Dim, and Endearingly Loyal.**
    Brandon doesn’t solve problems; he removes them with excessive force. He is permanently stuck in a pose that showcases his deltoids and regards his body as a singular, peak athletic temple. He’s the sweetest guy you'll meet... right up until you make fun of his jersey collection. He genuinely believes his flexing can defuse any crisis.

***

#### 4. **"Vibe" Veronica**
* **Role:** Pop Star, Motivational (and Musical) Specialist
* **Job:** Singing backup for her own inner monologue and being the face of "Vibes" globally.
* **Personality: Egotistical, Energetic, and Accidentally Profound.**
    Veronica is the definition of a pop diva. With her hot pink streak and her leather jacket, she approaches every situation with her microphone in hand. Her life is a nonstop music video, and she is both the star and the director. Every "Vibe check!" she calls is a mandatory moment of introspection. She may be shallow, but she *means* well... as long as you're a fan.

***

### The Bottom Row

***

#### 5. **Princess "Posh" Penelope**
* **Role:** Hereditary Monarch & Royal Decorum Coordinator
* **Job:** Advising on protocol, maintaining "the standard," and waiting for her kingdom to accept her back (she’s exiled).
* **Personality: Demanding, Elitist, and Constantly Shocked.**
    Penelope is a princess without a kingdom, but she is NOT without rules. She wears her silver tiara with severe importance and is perpetually *gasping* at the casual, peasant-like behavior of her teammates. To her, chaos is just a polite word for "not having enough gold forks." Her defining trait is her extreme sense of entitlement, which she considers to be "royal bearing."

***

#### 6. **"Control" Colin**
* **Role:** Strategic Overwatch & Tactical Esports Command
* **Job:** Coordinating missions from the "front line" (his bedroom) and achieving the highest score.
* **Personality: Intense, Serious, and Severely Introverted.**
    Colin doesn’t leave his command chair. With his high-tech headset and his mechanical gaming controller, he manages the team via satellite and encrypted voice comms. He views real-world missions as "just another level to clear," analyzing data points and screaming call-outs that his non-gamer teammates rarely understand. His "focus stare" is legendary... mostly because it's terrifyingly vacant.

***

#### 7. **The "Clown" Carl**
* **Role:** Emotional Well-being, Entertainment, and Psychological Warfare
* **Job:** Making people laugh (or scream in terror) and generally causing mischief.
* **Personality: Chaotic, Unhinged, and Desperately Needing Validation.**
    Carl is the quintessential clown. He’s not a *bad* clown, he’s just… a lot. He has a wide, non-blinking smile, bright orange hair, and a rainbow tie he never loosens. He uses classic jokes (pies, honking noses) at the worst possible moments and is always on the hunt for a punchline, even if the "joke" is at your expense. He can’t stop, won’t stop, because "the show must go on!"

***

#### 8. **The "Scribe" Simon**
* **Role:** Librarian, Historian, and Master of Trivia
* **Job:** Recording history and knowing exactly *why* every decision you make is flawed.
* **Personality: Anxious, Pedantic, and Book-Addicted.**
    Simon is a walking encyclopedia, drowning in a perpetual pile of dusty tomes. He wears taped-together glasses because he can’t stop dropping them while buried in a manuscript. He exists in a state of advanced panic because he’s always behind on his reading. He rarely speaks in full sentences, but when he does, it’s a 15-minute explanation of obscure protocol that the team ignored 10 minutes ago.
